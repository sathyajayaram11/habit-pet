# Credits

## Sound effects

Animal sounds are sourced from Wikimedia Commons under the **CC0 1.0 (Public Domain)** license — free to use, no attribution required. Listed here for provenance:

- **Dog (bark)** — from the CC0-Public-Domain-Sounds collection, CC0
  https://github.com/lavenderdotpet/CC0-Public-Domain-Sounds
- **Elephant (trumpet)** — "dragon-studio-elephant-trumpeting" from Pixabay
  (Pixabay Content License — free for commercial use, no attribution required)
- **Cat, Cow, Rabbit, Parrot** — user-provided clips, trimmed to a maximum of 3
  seconds and re-encoded to AAC (.m4a) for size and browser compatibility.

All clips are kept at 3 seconds or under for snappy playback.
